Odisha

Discover the cultural and historical richness of Odisha, a state known for its ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and diverse landscapes.

Key Highlights:

  • Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Konark Sun Temple, known for its architectural grandeur.
  • Visit the famous Jagannath Temple in Puri, a major pilgrimage site.
  • Experience the vibrant Rath Yatra festival celebrated with grand processions.
  • Explore the serene beaches of Puri and the wildlife of Chilika Lake.

Odisha offers a blend of spirituality, history, and natural beauty, making it a unique destination in East India.
