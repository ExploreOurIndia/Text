Jaipur

Discover the regal beauty of Jaipur, the Pink City, known for its magnificent palaces, vibrant markets, and rich cultural heritage.

Key Highlights:

  • Visit the iconic Hawa Mahal, a palace with unique pink sandstone architecture.
  • Explore the grandeur of the City Palace and its impressive museum.
  • Wander through the vibrant markets of Jaipur, such as Johari Bazaar.
  • Experience the majesty of Amer Fort, a hilltop fortress with panoramic views.

Jaipur offers a captivating blend of history, art, and traditional Rajasthani culture.
