Mysore

Discover the royal heritage of Mysore, a city known for its grand palace, vibrant markets, and cultural richness.

Key Highlights:

  • Explore the opulent Mysore Palace, a symbol of royal splendor.
  • Stroll through the colorful Devaraja Market for a taste of local life.
  • Visit the Chamundi Hills to witness the panoramic view of the city.
  • Experience the rich tradition of Dasara, Mysore's grand festival.

Mysore offers a captivating journey into history, art, and royal grandeur.
