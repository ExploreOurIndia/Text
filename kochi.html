Kochi

Experience the cultural diversity of Kochi, a city with Chinese fishing nets, colonial charm, and a rich historical background.

Key Highlights:

  • Explore Fort Kochi with its colonial architecture.
  • Visit the historic Mattancherry Palace.
  • Witness the unique Chinese fishing nets along the coast.
  • Indulge in the vibrant local seafood and diverse cuisine.
