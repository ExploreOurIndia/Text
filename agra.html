Discover Agra - Home to the Taj Mahal

Welcome to Agra, a city rich in history and culture, and renowned for the breathtaking Taj Mahal.

Explore the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Other Attractions in Agra

While the Taj Mahal is the highlight, Agra has more to offer:

  • Agra Fort: Explore the historical Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with a rich past.
  • Fatehpur Sikri: Visit the nearby Fatehpur Sikri, a city known for its well-preserved Mughal architecture.

Local Cuisine

Don't miss out on Agra's delicious cuisine. Try the famous Agra Petha and indulge in Mughlai dishes.

Plan Your Visit

If you're planning a trip to Agra, make sure to check the weather and plan your visit during the best time of the year. Consider a sunrise or sunset visit to the Taj Mahal for a magical experience.
