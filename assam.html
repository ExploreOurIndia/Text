Assam

Discover the natural beauty and rich culture of Assam, a state known for its tea gardens, wildlife sanctuaries, and traditional festivals.

Key Highlights:

  • Explore the lush tea estates of Assam, known for its world-famous Assam tea.
  • Visit Kaziranga National Park, home to the one-horned rhinoceros.
  • Experience the vibrant Bihu festival, celebrated with traditional dance and music.
  • Take a cruise on the Brahmaputra River and enjoy the scenic landscapes.

Assam offers a unique blend of nature, wildlife, and cultural festivities, providing a memorable experience in Northeast India.
