Sikkim

Discover the enchanting landscapes and cultural richness of Sikkim, a state known for its Himalayan beauty, monasteries, and adventure opportunities.

Key Highlights:

  • Explore the capital city Gangtok, surrounded by mountains and monasteries.
  • Visit the iconic Rumtek Monastery, a center of Tibetan Buddhism.
  • Embark on a trek to Nathula Pass for breathtaking views of the Himalayas.
  • Experience the serene beauty of Tsomgo Lake, surrounded by snow-capped peaks.

Sikkim offers a peaceful retreat in the lap of the Himalayas, with a mix of spirituality and adventure.
