Discover Leh Ladakh - Explore the Himalayas

Welcome to Leh Ladakh, a region known for its mesmerizing landscapes and vibrant culture.

Explore the Himalayan Beauty

Leh Ladakh, situated in the northernmost part of India, offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Explore the high mountain passes, serene lakes, and ancient monasteries.

Must-Visit Places

Make sure to include these places in your Leh Ladakh itinerary:

  • Pangong Lake: Witness the enchanting beauty of Pangong Lake, known for its changing colors.
  • Nubra Valley: Experience the unique landscapes of Nubra Valley and its sand dunes.
  • Thiksey Monastery: Visit the ancient Thiksey Monastery for a spiritual experience.

Adventure Activities

Leh Ladakh is a paradise for adventure enthusiasts. Try activities like trekking, river rafting, and biking amidst the stunning scenery.

Local Culture and Cuisine

Immerse yourself in the rich Ladakhi culture. Explore local markets and savor traditional Ladakhi cuisine, including momos and thukpa.

Plan Your Adventure

Ensure you acclimatize to the high altitude and plan your visit during the favorable weather months. Pack accordingly for the cold climate.
