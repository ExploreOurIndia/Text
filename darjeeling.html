Darjeeling

Discover the picturesque landscapes and cultural charm of Darjeeling, a hill station known for its tea gardens, stunning views of the Himalayas, and colonial architecture.

Key Highlights:

  • Visit the iconic Tiger Hill to witness a breathtaking sunrise over the Himalayas.
  • Explore the lush tea estates of Darjeeling and learn about the tea-making process.
  • Ride the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Stroll through the vibrant markets of Mall Road and enjoy local Tibetan cuisine.

Darjeeling offers a serene escape into nature with a blend of colonial history and cultural diversity.
