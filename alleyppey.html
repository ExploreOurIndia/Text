Alleppey

Experience the enchanting backwaters of Alleppey, often referred to as the "Venice of the East," known for its serene houseboat cruises and picturesque landscapes.

Key Highlights:

  • Embark on a houseboat cruise through the tranquil backwaters.
  • Witness the beauty of paddy fields and traditional villages along the waterways.
  • Experience the unique lifestyle of the locals living by the backwaters.
  • Enjoy traditional Kerala cuisine served on the houseboat.

Alleppey offers a perfect blend of nature and cultural immersion, providing a peaceful retreat.
