Discover Shimla - Experience the Queen of Hills

Welcome to Shimla, the charming hill station known for its colonial architecture, scenic beauty, and pleasant weather.

Explore the Queen of Hills

Shimla offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and historical charm. Explore the colonial-era architecture and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Must-Visit Places

Make sure to include these places in your Shimla itinerary:

  • The Ridge: Take a stroll along the famous Ridge, offering panoramic views of the Himalayas.
  • Jakhoo Temple: Visit the ancient Jakhoo Temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman.
  • Christ Church: Admire the neo-Gothic architecture of Christ Church, the second oldest church in North India.

Adventure and Nature

Experience adventure activities like trekking and skiing in the nearby hills. Enjoy the lush greenery and serene landscapes of Shimla's surroundings.

Local Culture and Cuisine

Explore the local markets, indulge in Himachali cuisine, and try popular dishes like sidu and chha meat.

Plan Your Getaway

Plan your visit during the summer for pleasant weather or in winter for a chance to experience snowfall. Don't miss the Shimla Summer Festival if you're visiting in May.
