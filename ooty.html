Ooty

Discover the serene beauty of Ooty, the "Queen of the Nilgiris," known for its picturesque landscapes and pleasant climate.

Key Highlights:

  • Explore the lush tea gardens and learn about the tea-making process.
  • Enjoy a scenic ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.
  • Stroll through the beautiful Botanical Gardens.
  • Experience the tranquility of Ooty Lake.

Whether it's the captivating views or the charming colonial-era architecture, Ooty promises a delightful escape into nature.
