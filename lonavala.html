Lonavala

Experience the natural beauty of Lonavala, a hill station known for its lush landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and serene surroundings.

Key Highlights:

  • Visit the scenic viewpoints like Tiger Point and Duke's Nose for breathtaking vistas.
  • Explore the ancient Karla and Bhaja Caves, showcasing Buddhist rock-cut architecture.
  • Enjoy a leisurely walk around Lonavala Lake surrounded by greenery.
  • Indulge in the local Chikki, a popular sweet treat made with jaggery and nuts.

Lonavala offers a tranquil escape into nature, making it a perfect destination for relaxation and exploration.
